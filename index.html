About

Nathan Phillip Smith has been teaching piano lessons in Orem, UT for over a decade. He got his start in piano at a young age and traveled internationally to perform in Chile, Peru, Germany, Austria, and South Africa. Nathan competed in and placed in competitions throughout his youth and is a former board member of the Utah International Youth Piano Competition. Nathan's most current work includes studying piano performance at Utah Valley University, and releasing his first album, Beautiful Day. He has been played on the radio both locally and internationally.
