About

Nathan Phillip Smith has been teaching piano lessons in Orem, UT for over a decade. He got his start in piano at a young age and traveled internationally to perform in Chile, Peru, Germany, Austria, and South Africa. Nathan competed in and placed in competitions throughout his youth and is a former board member of the Utah International Youth Piano Competition. Nathan's most current work includes studying piano performance at Utah Valley University, and releasing his first album, Beautiful Day. He has been played on the radio both locally and internationally. Nathan is a registered teacher through Music Teachers National Association (MTNA). His belief is that a successful pianist should learn repertoire, music theory, technique, music history, sight reading, and ear training. These areas are the focus for each lesson in the studio. Nathan believes that with these pillars of learning students will not only progress in their skill, but foster a love for the piano and good music. We look forward to meeting with you to determine our compatibility as student and teacher. Thank you!
